Creative Digital Media

In the modern world, digital media is part of our lives. Creative digital media is helping to bring an innovation in every field. Creative Digital Media is a more about technologies, content and building digital media products that requires teams of professionals with diverse skills, including technical skills, artistic skills, analytical and production coordination skills etc.
Digital Media content can be transmitted over the internet or computer networks. Digital Media is translating analog data into digital data which means machine readable format.

Digital media can be created, viewed, distributed, modified and preserved on digital electronics devices.This can include software, digital images, digital video, video game, web pages and websites, including social media, data and databases, digital audio, such as MP3 and electronic books.
Digital media has also allowed individuals to be much more active in content creation. This quality of digital media has led to significant changes in many industries, especially journalism, publishing, education, healthcare, entertainment, sports and the music business. It is easy to copy, store, share and modify.
